Phishing Awareness

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers attempt to trick individuals into providing sensitive information.

Common Tactics

Phishers often use fake emails and websites that mimic legitimate organizations to steal personal information.

How to Spot Phishing

Look for suspicious links, unusual email addresses, and unsolicited requests for information.
